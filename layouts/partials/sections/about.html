About the symposium

NECB 2026 brings together researchers from across New England — and beyond — working at the intersection of computation, machine learning, and the life sciences. Across two days of keynotes, contributed talks, open-problem sessions, tutorials, and poster presentations, the symposium aims to spark new collaborations and accelerate progress on the field's most exciting open questions.

The symposium is organized by a steering committee of computational biologists from local universities and research institutes, with the goal of being affordable, accessible, and welcoming to trainees.
