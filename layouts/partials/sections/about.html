About the symposium

NECB 2026 brings together the New England computational biology community for two days of keynotes, contributed talks, open-problem sessions, tutorials, and posters at Microsoft Research Cambridge.

We are an in-person, locally rooted symposium with a few simple goals: make it easy for researchers across New England universities, hospitals, and institutes to meet each other; create visibility for junior researchers and trainees; and seed new collaborations at the frontier of computation and the life sciences.

Registration is handled by ISCB and is kept intentionally affordable so that students, postdocs, and early-career investigators can attend. Outstanding poster presentations will receive awards.
